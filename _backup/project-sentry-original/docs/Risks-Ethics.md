Risks & Ethics

Safety Disclaimer
- The app is an assistive tool, not a guarantee.
- It may miss crashes or trigger false alerts.
- It does not automatically contact emergency services.

Privacy
- Sensor data is stored locally by default.
- Users must opt-in before any data is shared.
- Emergency contacts are managed on-device.

User Experience
- Alerts should be clear and large to reduce confusion.
- Avoid prompts that distract the driver.

Risk Mitigation
- Use a confirmation step before any alert is sent.
- Provide quick cancellation if false alarm occurs.
